Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Information We Collect

DropDeadFrugal is an automated bot that:

  • Monitors publicly available Amazon product information
  • Posts deal information to Twitter
  • Does not collect personal user data
  • Does not store user information from Twitter interactions

How We Use Information

We use publicly available product data solely to:

  • Identify beauty product deals and discounts
  • Share deal information with our Twitter followers
  • Generate affiliate links for qualifying purchases

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute any personal data. All information we work with is:

  • Publicly available product information from Amazon
  • Shared only through our public Twitter account
  • Used solely for the purpose of finding and sharing deals

Affiliate Disclosure

DropDeadFrugal participates in the Amazon Associates Program. We may earn a small commission from qualifying purchases made through our affiliate links, at no additional cost to you.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through Twitter @DropDeadFrugal.
